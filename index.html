On the other hand on some occasion I have failed in these tasks which have not caused me to bow my head but to raise it higher in order to continue to learn and improve myself.

By taking on these tasks I have learned to adapt in difficult circumstances and have also learned to look onward and not just at the task at hand. I am aware of that many times the result will not be in my favor but knowing that I was able to get an opportunity in any profession allows me to acknowledge that perhaps the next time the result will improve. That has always been my goal not to succeed and take glory but to acknowledge my mistakes in order to form them into victories.

I am aware that I am still at a young age before I can really call myself a professional. My eagerness to continue to grow will not only allow employers to seek my employment but allow me to be placed in a career in which I can help others benefit. To conclude the attitude and the eagerness in which I apply to everyday tasks will not only allow me to contribute to my career and work my way up the ladder to perhaps a managerial position.
